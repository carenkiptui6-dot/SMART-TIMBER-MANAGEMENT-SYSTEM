This is a Smart Timber Yard Management System built using Object Orinted Programmng Principles in C++. Many Timber Yards, sawmills and timber stores still manage timer stock manually using notebooks or loose records which leads to poor tracking of stock, wrong pricing, loss of sales records, inaccuracy in record keeping because of human generated errors and difficulty in identifying timber types and quantities. Manual records also lead to duplication of records, loss or damage of records making data management unreliable.
I came up with this idea as i thought of a timber yard that i usually see when coming from school where the timber are stacked and there are workers who manually count the timer and sell them and i guess they use books to make records because i have never seen a computerized system in that place hence i thought that the C++ principles that we have been taught are of great help to reduce duplication of records and mitigate the act of overcharging and undercharging of timber prices, putting into consideration the OOP principles such as inheritance, encapsulation and polymorphism are of a great help in such real world situation.
 Hence the Smart Timber Yard System that i came up with built in C++ language principles will help in  eliminating the duplication of records, loss of records, human generated errors and also increases efficiency and brings a high sense of organization and at the end leads to more potential customers.
